Hobbies

Hockey

As a Division I hockey goaltender, hockey is my main passion. My schedule revolves around practices, workouts, film sessions, and travel for games. Playing at this level taught me resilience, teamwork, and quick decision-making. Off the ice I study opponent tendencies, work with coaches on positioning, and focus on recovery to stay at my best.

Fishing

Fishing is where I recharge. Early mornings on Minnesota lakes are peaceful and give me a chance to reflect between the intensity of the season. I enjoy both ice fishing in winter and open-water trips in summer.
